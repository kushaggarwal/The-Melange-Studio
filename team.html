Meet Our Team

Anjali Aggarwal

Director and Design Principal

Anjali Aggarwal has over 3 years of professional experience working & practicing in the field of Interior Design, Planning, and Execution in Educational Universities, Master Planning and Townships during her work exposure at Vastushilpa consultants based in Ahmedabad. She is adept at building strong relationships with clients, as well as bringing together design and technical skills. As an architect, she strongly believes in inventive thinking and research driven design approach, anchored to uncompromising ethics, pro-active participation in resolving issues, creating dialogues between nature, humans & built environment. By her meticulous involvement through the delivery of the project, she combines imagination and sound practical sense with an eye for detail.

Pranvi Jain

Director and Design Principal

Pranvi is a graduate from the University School of Architecture and planning (Delhi) She has worked extensively for 3+ years at Anagram Architects & Studio Lotus, Delhi across a range of residential architecture, hospitality projects and commercial sectors encompassing all stages of the design and procurement process. She has an eye for good design and believes that simplicity is the ultimate sophistication.The process of architecture according to her requires a narrative-based experience design till the very end detail of the project.   

Dhruv

Director and Design Principal

Dhruv’s career has been shaped by his passion for improving the civic realm—the places and buildings that represent a diversified culture of our society. He has a well-versed understanding of the creative process stemming from his 3 years of experience at Chromed Design Studio, Delhi in the Restaurants & Hospitality Sector, Retail Spaces, Corporate Interiors, Brand identities for hospitality projects, Installation and Graphic Design with an emphasis on innovation and brand presence to shape definitive projects at the practice. He believes in experimentation but within the realms to explore purposeful architecture through various techniques, delivering meaningful buildings that exhibit a message of cultural relevance. His ideation of bringing playfulness, vibrancy, and quirk to enhance the spatial narrative adds an out-of-the-box asset to our studio.
