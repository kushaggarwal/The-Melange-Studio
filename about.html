About Us

01
Vision

We believe that design is about more than meeting people’s needs—it’s the art of realizing their imagination and changing the way they experience the world. For us, the best results come from a balance of disciplines: planning interiors, engineering & architecture. Right from creating the initial design to building the final product, we develop conceptual designs through end to end details. Our team foresees the work flexibly across a range of sectors, disciplines, and locations (From large scale urban projects to residential and commercial interior projects- we cater it all). We combine the expertise of our team to create unique, responsive designs and successful client and partner relationships.

02

“The Melange Studio” is an interdisciplinary approach that believes in delivering transformative solutions through a value driven process. Founded by Anjali Aggarwal, Dhruv and Pranvi Jain in 2020, we pursue a co-creative design process by working on projects ranging from architecture to interiors, we at melange studio are always motivated to transform from what is conventional into something more unique.
